
# [What is Data Recovery?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=umMKTr2OlIU&t=4s)


Data recovery is the process of restoring lost or corrupted data after an unexpected event. This can occur due to physical damage to a device, malicious actions by attackers, or malware. The method of recovery depends on the nature of the data loss and the availability of backups.

Data recovery software can be used to analyze damaged hardware like hard disks or flash drives to extract files. If backups exist, lost data can be restored from them.

For IT professionals, data recovery is crucial for maintaining business operations. By ensuring data availability and protection, IT specialists minimize disruptions. Key strategies include:

- **Disaster Planning:** Developing a comprehensive plan that involves regular backups of critical data such as customer information, databases, system configurations, and financial records.
- **Data Backups:** Regularly backing up all essential data is the most effective way to prepare for data loss.
- **Post-Mortem Analysis:** After a data loss event and recovery, documenting any issues encountered and their solutions helps prevent future occurrences and improve disaster plans.
