These are all in one solutions that help you track user issues, communicate with your users and provide updates.

A great way to use the system for documentation is to update the ticket with what the issue is.

The **steps and procedures** you're trying to resolve and the solution you arrived at.

You want to be as **concise** as possible so that when someone reads your document, they can easily figure out what they need to do.
