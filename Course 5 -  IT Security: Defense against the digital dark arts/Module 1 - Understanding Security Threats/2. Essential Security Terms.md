
# [Essential Security Terms](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zwjNZWWb3Nc&t=4s)

### Key Terminology:

- **Risk:** The possibility of suffering a loss or an attack on a system. For example, not setting a screen lock on a phone creates a risk of data theft.

<img width="1280" height="397" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b4e9d62c-c71d-4ce5-86bf-3e4b7b7b0094" />

- **Vulnerability:** A flaw in a system that can be exploited. An example is leaving a bathroom window unlocked when leaving for vacation, or forgetting to disable a debug account in a web app before launch.
  - **Zero-day Vulnerability:** A vulnerability unknown to the software vendor but known to an attacker, giving the vendor zero days to fix it.

<img width="1279" height="376" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/524673ac-1a27-41c6-af7c-f13becb0ca89" />

<img width="1280" height="426" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9c3707ed-8349-441d-a9d4-2a126fcb0a4c" />

- **Exploit:** Software used to take advantage of a security bug or vulnerability. Attackers write exploit code, including zero-day exploits, to cause harm.

<img width="1279" height="405" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/953460d3-34bc-4fe1-8193-f6b78832f0ad" />

- **Threat:** The potential for danger that could exploit a vulnerability, essentially a possible attacker.

<img width="1271" height="454" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9fcb3227-6f48-40d3-8e2f-cf6a31b2c8ce" />

- **Hacker:** Someone who attempts to break into or exploit a system.
  - **Black Hat Hackers:** Malicious actors seeking to harm systems.
  - **White Hat Hackers:** Ethical hackers who find weaknesses and report them to owners for fixing.

<img width="1277" height="455" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/79c78491-a7e3-456a-bb7a-2f92e2105d71" />

<img width="1279" height="655" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4aa1bc4d-7b86-4126-b78d-abce185d40d8" />

- **Attack:** An actual attempt to cause harm to a system.
